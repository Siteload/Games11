About

Bottle Flip is a fun casual game where you need to click on the screen to flip a bottle to the top of many different household items. The longer you hold your click, the further the bottle will flip. Beware not to flip the bottle too close or too far that it lands on the floor! Flip the bottle as many times as possible to get the most points, and collect the blue diamonds to buy new bottles and cosmetics! Have fun!
